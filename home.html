Powering Urban Mobility With Data




OUR PRODUCT




Substantial growth in number of vehicles and limited road infrastructure has caused rapidly growing vehicular congestion in Indian cities. This has lead to increasing level of air pollution, fuel wastage and decrease in productivity due to man-hours lost at traffic.


SmaTraffi is an intelligent adaptive signal solution, that uses real-time vehicular data along with predictive analytics and optimization to solve the problem of avoidable traffic delays and congestion at traffic junctions. It uses an out-of-the-box and innovative technique to calculate signal timers while solving various objectives, such as minimizing average wait time or maximizing number of cars leaving a junction.

01/ SMART

  • SmaTraffi is built on a trusted and reliable technology, which gives accurate results
  • It uses real time data along with statistical predictive models to come up with signal timers.
  • It is a completely automated solution, requiring no human intervention, thus avoiding human errors.
02/ FEASIBLE

  • SmaTraffi is a cloud-based solution, therefore not needing any hardware devices to be installed
  • It requires only a week to calibrate according to traffic data.
  • It can be easily integrated with the existing traffic architecture.
  • Updates and changes can be done remotely.
03/ ECONOMICAL

  • SmaTraffi does not require any physical assets, and hence avoids any fixed costs.
  • It does not require high computational processing costs, like GPU's.
  • No additional maintenance cost.
  • It is 5-7 times cheaper than traditional adaptive signal systems.
